Meet Skye

Chef. Artist. Host. The one who turns a meal into an experience.

Skye reads the room before she touches the stove. Working closely with you, Skye crafts your menu, your bar, and an atmosphere built around your guests, your space, and the story of your night. Some dishes are prepped ahead for flawless execution; others are finished at your table - because the sight, sound, and smell of cooking is part of your experience.

With a career spanning fine dining, art curation, and live music, Skye delivers an experience that is bespoke and tailored around you and your guests. Private dinners, gallery nights, or warehouse parties - she helps you make memories.
